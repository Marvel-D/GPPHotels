about Green paradise

Green Paradise Palms Hotels was conceived out of the passion to enhance the lives of the community and to give a facelift to the origin of my birthplace. It took us five years of rigorous approach in making the dream come true. The hotel was given attention in making the place as attractive as European standard in terms of facility, services, environment, security and being the best in the environment. We are determined to be growing each year in order to meet up with the demand and expectations of our customers and in fulfilling our dreams in a larger form.
